Pokémon Battle!

Welcome to Pokémon Battle, the only simplified simulation of an imaginary Pokémon battle anywhere on the Web! Please start by creating a Pokémon from the prompts below, and take them into battle against a randomly generated enemy! After the battle, you can choose to play again with the same Pokémon to try against a different enemy, or you can create a whole new one!

The creation process is as follows: first, name your Pokémon! Then, choose that Pokémon's type from the drop down list! Lastly, select a build for your Pokémon's stats! Choose among Strong, Speedy, Bulky, and Balanced! Then click the fight button to initiate the battle!

Your turn is to reduce the enemy's Hit Points to zero before they can do the same to your Pokémon! The enemy Pokémon's type will be displayed, so be sure to take advantage of type matchups! The Pokémon with highest speed goes first! On your turn, you will be able to select one of four moves! You will alternate turns with the enemy until either of your Pokémon has fainted!

Have fun!

Moveset:
Attack: Hit the opposing Pokémon with an attack of the same type as your Pokémon
Punch: Hit the opposing Pokémon with a weaker, typeless attack
Howl: Raise your Pokémon's attack stat
Harden: Raise your Pokémon's defense stat
